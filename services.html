Our services

Carpentry, end to end.

From a single door fitment to a full timber-frame build — every project is handled on-site with the same care and attention.

Roofing

New roof installations, repairs and replacements. Trusses, beams, battens, ridging and waterproofing — built to handle whatever Cape weather throws at it.

Timber-frame building

Full timber-frame structures — granny flats, holiday cabins, family homes, garden offices. Energy-efficient, fast to build and beautiful inside and out.

Door fitment

Hanging, fitting and finishing internal and external doors of every kind — solid wood, composite, security gates and pivots — with hardware properly installed.

Flooring

Hardwood, laminate and engineered timber flooring laid level, square and finished to last. Sub-floor preparation, skirting and trim included.

Decking

Custom decks, patios, walkways and pool surrounds in hardwood and composite. Designed for South African UV, salt air and rainfall.

Additions & alterations

Extending what you already love — new rooms, second storeys, knock-throughs and full renovations, project-managed from first sketch to final coat of paint.

Building plans & designs

Plans drawn up and submitted, ready for council approval. We work with trusted draughtsmen and architects so your project starts on the right foundation.
